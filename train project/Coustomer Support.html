Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a train ticket?

To book a train ticket, simply visit our booking page, select your destination, date, and seat preference, and proceed with payment.

How do I cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking through your account dashboard. There is a cancellation fee based on the time before departure.

What should I do if my train is delayed?

If your train is delayed, you will be notified via email or SMS. You can also check the real-time status on our website.

Is Change of boarding point permissible ?

Yes, facility to change boarding point is available on IRCTC website.

Can Concessional tickets be booked ?

Yes, Senior Citizen Concession, Divyaang Concession and Journalist Concessions are allowed on booking tickets on IRCTC as per extant Railway rules.
