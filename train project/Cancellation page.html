Cancellation Policy

At TrainBook, we offer flexible cancellation options, depending on the timing of your cancellation. Below are the details:

  • Up to 24 hours before departure: Full refund available minus a small processing fee.
  • Within 24 hours but before 12 hours of departure: 50% refund of the total fare.
  • Less than 12 hours before departure: No refund available.
  • Special cases: In the event of train delays or cancellations by the operator, full refunds are provided.

By proceeding with the cancellation, you agree to the terms and conditions of our cancellation policy as mentioned above.
